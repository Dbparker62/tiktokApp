# Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 23, 2026**

## Terms of Service

This tool is a private, personal desktop application used solely by its developer. 
It is not available to the public and has no end users.

The application automates video content creation and publishing to social media platforms 
including TikTok and YouTube using official APIs.

By using this tool you agree that:
- You are the sole operator and user of this application
- You will comply with TikTok's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
- You will only publish content to accounts you own and control
- You will not use this tool to spam, mislead, or violate any platform policies

## Privacy Policy

This application is a private personal tool with no public users.

**Data collected:** None. No user data is collected, stored, or shared with any third party.

**Authentication tokens:** OAuth tokens for TikTok and YouTube are stored locally on your 
own machine in the project directory. They are never transmitted to any external server 
other than the official TikTok and YouTube APIs.

**Video content:** Videos are generated locally and uploaded directly to TikTok and YouTube 
via their official APIs. No content is stored or processed by any third party.
