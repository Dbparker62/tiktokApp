Terms of Service

This is a private personal desktop application used solely by its developer. It is not available to the public and has no end users.

This application is a private personal tool with no public users.

Data collected: None. No user data is collected, stored, or shared with any third party.

Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens are stored locally on your own machine and are never transmitted to any server other than the official TikTok and YouTube APIs.

Video content: Videos are generated locally and uploaded directly to TikTok and YouTube via their official APIs.
